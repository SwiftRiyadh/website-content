## Welcome to Swift Riyadh! 🎉

Join us for the very first Swift Riyadh meetup! This is a kickoff event where we'll introduce the community, share our vision, and get to know fellow Swift developers in the city.

### What to Expect

- **Welcome & Introduction** — Meet the organizers and learn about our plans for 2026
- **Lightning Talks** — Quick 5-minute talks from community members
- **Networking** — Connect with fellow Swift developers over refreshments
- **Q&A Session** — Share your ideas for future events and topics

### Who Should Attend?

Whether you're a seasoned iOS developer, a Swift beginner, or just curious about the language — everyone is welcome! This is a community for all skill levels.

See you there! 🧡
