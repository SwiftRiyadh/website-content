## Why Swift Riyadh?

Riyadh is booming. The tech scene is growing faster than ever, fueled by Vision 2030, massive investments in digital infrastructure, and a generation of talented developers ready to build the future.

Yet, despite the incredible growth, there hasn't been a dedicated space for Swift developers in the city. That changes today.

**Swift Riyadh** is a community by developers, for developers. We're here to:

- 🤝 **Connect** — Bring together iOS developers, Swift enthusiasts, and anyone curious about Apple's powerful language
- 📚 **Learn** — Host workshops, talks, and study groups for all skill levels
- 🚀 **Build** — Collaborate on open source projects and support each other's work
- 🌍 **Represent** — Put Riyadh on the map in the global Swift community

### What's Coming

We have exciting plans for 2026:

- **Monthly meetups** at awesome venues across Riyadh
- **Hands-on workshops** covering SwiftUI, Vapor, Swift concurrency, and more
- **Hackathons** to build cool things together
- **Open source** initiatives contributing to the Swift ecosystem
